Greetings! I'm Jennifer, a self taught Front-End Web Developer based in Knoxville, Tennessee. I've always had an interest in coding, but lacked the motivation and courage to try for the longest time; for fear of it being 'too hard'. As I've found out, it is hard- but it is also fun! I hope to continue my learning and become a professional Front-End Dev... so be on the lookout for me!

My favorite pastimes are usually centered around video games and anime. I also draw, paint, write romance novels and read a lot! (Of romance novels, ha ha). You can say I have my hand in a lot of hobby cookie jars. I have a great husband named Max, two cats named Gwen and Morgana, and a dog named Taco.

Outside of coding I work as a full-time dog groomer! As a result I have developed a passion for animals and always look to educate people on how to properly take care of their dog/cat. I also was diagnosed with Autism/OCD as an adult and made a world of difference in my life when I finally began to get treated for it. Now I advocate for others to do the same! Find out who you truly are, life doesn't have to be so hard!
